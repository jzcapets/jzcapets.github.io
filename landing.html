Life-Saving Technology

This mouse is cancer-free thanks to Ramicane(TM)

Are you or your pet suffering from a tiny case of cancer? Squeakers, pictured left, is now 22-days cancer-free thanks to the life-saving drug Ramicane. Stage-1 clinical trials indicate that Ramicane (TM) is the most effective drug currently being studied as a treatment for mouse cancer. Cancer is the leading cause of mouse death in mice over the age of 40 (days).
